FlapScore

FlapScore is a Flappy Bird Score screenshot generator. It's simple, just enter a score there and it will give you a 1080p screenshot of the game with that score. It includes some randomizing on the background and correct medal based on score. You can simply take the screenshot and impress your friends, but yeah, keep it good, no one would ever believe a score of 9483922 but could believe a score of 120.

Go try it: FlapScore (short: git.io/flapscore)

Oh and, this was just a pass-time project because I liked Flappy Bird but I hated it at the same time because it was too difficult to make score there. Don't expect me to update it, this may go down if there is high load on MySong server because of this.
