Squishy Bird

Squishy Bird is a parody of Flappy Bird, which was an utterly popular yet impossible-to-win game. This is the Windows Phone version of the same.

Playing this game is simple enough, you just have to tap the screen to kill the birds coming forward using the pipes. As you close the pipe, if the bird just clashes and moves away, it becomes reddish and these birds give you more points, but they are faster too!

If you wanna know stats on this app, you can check them here. Just in case you wanna know, this app had over 400,000 downloads with 200,000 in first 2 weeks itself. This was also listed on Gizmodo's "Best apps of the Week".
