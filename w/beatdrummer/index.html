The Beat Drummer

Beat Drummer is a HTML5 web based implementation of a beat box. Includes 14 most important and popular tones to make your own music and start sharing it too.

Beat Drummer is built such a way that your sounds can be shared easily with your friends, you just have to share a URL with them.

Go try it: Beat Drummer (short: git.io/drummer)

I worked on this project when I was really bored (and I didn't have an internet connection), later on one of my friend made a better UI for this.

Did I mention this is an open-source app? You can fork it and make it suite your needs. (If you want to make it better, just submit a pull request). Get it: github.com/akshatmittal/beatdrummer
