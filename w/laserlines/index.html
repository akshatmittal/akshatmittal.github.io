Laser Lines

Laser Lines is a 'connect the lasers' kind of game. There are circular laser generaters that connect to every other laser generator with a straight line (called as 'laser' inside the game). All you have to do is to arrange the generators so that no 2 lasers cross each other. Starting levels are easy with increasing difficulty as you move forward. Includes total of 30 levels to play.
