MySong

MySong is a simple web based app that helps you to download any song by just searching and clicking 'download'. This initially supported only bollywood songs but later on, I updated it to support English music and partially World Music.

The site is hosted at MySong.ga, you can go and try it for yourself.

You can catch the updates on my profile or MySong's Facebook page.
